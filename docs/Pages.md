# Pages

## Authentication

-   **Login**: Sign in to your Jellyfin server.

## Core Library

-   **Home**: High-level dashboard with hero greetings, featured tracks, recently played cards, and quick playlist shortcuts
-   **Recently played**: Browse the recent listening history and replay or queue the entries.
-   **Playlists**: All playlists in your Jellyfin library in a grid/list view, with inline play controls.
-   **Albums / Artists / Genres / Tracks**: Shared browse UI with the list/grid toggle plus alphanumeric scroller, search access, and quick play controls.
-   **Favorites**: Separate grids for favorite albums, artists, and tracks for rapid access.
-   **Favorite Tracks**: Track-focused favorites view
-   **Offline**: Dedicated sections for cached albums, artists, playlists, and tracks when offline mode is enabled
-   **History**: Session play history
-   **Queue**: Full upcoming-tracks queue view
-   **Settings**: Adjust appearance, playback, cache, offline downloads, and other preferences.
-   **Search**: Persistent search overlay that focuses automatically when the keyboard shortcut or search button is pressed.

## Detail Views

-   **Playlist detail**: Hero artwork card, track listing, favorites, and offline toggles for an individual playlist.
-   **Smart list detail**: Smart list workflow detail with rule list, quick actions, and track queue.
-   **Album detail**: Artwork-heavy album view with metadata, favorite/playlist actions, and track rows.
-   **Artist detail**: Artist hero with biography, related albums, and the ability to queue or favorite any song.
-   **Genre detail**: Focused genre landing page that highlights the genre card and track list.
-   **Now Playing (expanded)**: Full-screen now playing theatre that emphasizes artwork, scrub controls, and queue links.
